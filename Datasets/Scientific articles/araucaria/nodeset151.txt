Tayside police's efforts to open desks in rural offices have stalled because the Post Office is asking far too much money. chief Constable John vine turned up the pressure on the organization yesterday by saying that, if it did not lower its demands, the force would pursue alternatives by trying to rent space in other village buildings ("Police unhappy at PO demands", The Courier, n. 47138, Friday, Agoust 27, 2004, p. 1)
