Mrs. A. Gardner discourages people from participating in the so-called AFP test, which measures the chances of a pregnant having a baby with spina bifida or Down s syndrome. Mrs. Gardner knows all about the consequences of the AFP test. So why did she have such a test done herself? Because in fact she preferred not to have a mongoloid baby?
